Rectus Femoris Action	EXTEND the knee and FLEX the hip
Vastus Lateralis Action	EXTEND the knee
Vastus Medialis Action	EXTEND the knee
Vastus Intermedius Action	EXTEND the knee
Sartorius Action	FLEX, LATERALLY ROTATE, and ABDUCT the HIP, and FLEX AND MEDIALLY ROTATE the flexed knee
Tensor Fasciae Latae (TFL) Action	FLEX, MEDIALLY ROTATE, and ABDUCT the hip
Petineus Action	ADDUCT and MEDIALLY ROTATE the hip; assist to FLEX the hip
Adductor Longus Action	ADDUCT and MEDIALLY ROTATE the hip; assist to FLEX the hip
Adductor Brevis Action	ADDUCT and MEDIALLY ROTATE the hip; assist to FLEX the hip
Adductor Magnus Action	ADDUCT and MEDIALLY ROTATE the hip; assist to FLEX the hip  Posterior Fibers: EXTEND the hip
Gracilis Action	ADDUCT and MEDIALLY ROTATE the hip, FLEX the knee, and MEDIALLY ROTATE the flexed knee
Semitendinosus Action	FLEX the knee, MEDIALLY ROTATE the flexed knee, EXTEND the hip, assist to MEDIALLY ROTATE the hip, and TILT the pelvis posteriorly
Semimembranosus Action	FLEX the knee, MEDIALLY ROTATE the flexed knee, EXTEND the hip, assist to MEDIALLY ROTATE the hip, and TILT the pelvis posteriorly
Biceps Femoris Action	FLEX the knee, LATERALLY ROTATE the flexed knee  Long head: EXTEND the hip, assist to LATERALLY ROTATE the hip
Gluteus Maximus Action	All fibers: EXTEND, LATERALLY ROTATE, and ABDUCT the hip  Lower fibers: ADDUCT the hip
Gluteus Medius Action	All fibers: ABDUCT the hip  Anterior fibers: FLEX and MEDIALLY ROTATE the hip  Posterior fibers: EXTEND and LATERALLY ROTATE the hip
Gluteus Minimus Action	ABDUCT, MEDAILLY ROTATE, and FLEX the hip
Piriformis Action	LATERALLY ROTATE the hip, ABDUCT the hip when the hip is flexed
Obturator Internus Action	LATERALLY ROTATE the hip
Obturator Externus Action	LATERALLY ROTATE the hip
Superior Gemellus Action	LATERALLY ROTATE the hip
Inferior Gemellus Action	LATERALLY ROTATE the hip
Quadratus Femoris Action	LATERALLY ROTATE the hip
